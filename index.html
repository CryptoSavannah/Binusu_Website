What are Binusu’s interest rates?

Your interest rate is determined by:

  1. Your Loyalty tier, which in turn depends on the ratio OF BINUSU Tokens against the balance of other assets in your portfolio. The requirements for each tier are as follows:
    • Base: No BINUSU Tokens are needed.
    • Silver: At least 1% of the Portfolio Balance in your account must comprise BINUSU Tokens.
    • Gold: At least 5% of the Portfolio Balance in your account must comprise BINUSU Tokens.
    • Platinum: At least 10% of the Portfolio Balance in your account must comprise BINUSU Tokens.
  2. Whether you choose to receive your interest in kind or Binusu Tokens:
    • Earning in kind means you receive your interest payments in the currency you’re earning on.
    • Earning in Binusu means you receive interest payments in BINUSU Tokens and benefit from up to 2% additional interest on all your holdings.

You can calculate your daily interest as shown below:

Daily Interest = Principal amount (in the original cryptocurrency) x (interest rate/365)

Note: Your interest rate will change depending on the ratio of Binusu Tokens to other assets in your portfolio and whether you decide to earn interest in Binusu or in kind.

What is loan-to-value (LTV)?

The loan-to-value (LTV) ratio is the ratio of the loan relative to the value of the collateral asset).

LTV is calculated as the loan amount in USDx divided by the value of the collateral in USDx, expressed as a percentage.

Example:

Credit amount: $5,000

Value of collateral: $10,000

Loan-to-Value = ($5,000/$10,000)*100% = 50.0%

For exact LTV on each asset, visit our help center.

Are there any fees?

No, there are no prepayment, origination or installment fees.

  • You can repay your outstanding balance, in part or completely, at any time and you only pay interest for the period you borrowed.
  • No minimum repayments.
  • No installments – you can make repayments at any time.

The interest you accrue on your credit is added to your outstanding balance daily at 22:00 UTC.

Note: If you repay a loan within 30 days of the last withdrawal, you will be charged interest for the full 30 days.

What happens if the value of my collateral changes before I repay my credit line?

What happens if the value of my collateral changes before I repay my credit line?

If your collateral appreciates in value, your credit line limit also goes up. You’re free to withdraw more credit or leave your current loan as is.

You can also use the extra value of your crypto to pay off part of your credit. If your assets have met your price target, then you can:

  • Place an order in your Binusu Wallet to sell a fraction of the crypto assets in your account.
  • Repay part or all of your outstanding balance.

The transaction is performed without any additional fees on Binusu’s part. Any resulting charges are fees from the exchange that facilitated the trade.

If your collateralized assets decrease in value and you do not add additional assets to your Binusu account after receiving warnings from the Binusu blockchain oracle, the Binusu blockchain oracle may initiate partial automatic loan repayments to rebalance the Loan-to-Value back within limits.

Can I borrow more than one time?

You can withdraw from your Binusu credit line as many times as you want as long as you:

  • Maintain the required loan-to-value ratio, and
  • Have enough collateral in your Binusu account to cover your credit.

Note: If there is not enough collateral in your Credit Line Wallet for a credit withdrawal, assets from your Savings Wallet will be transferred automatically to your Credit Line Wallet.

How do I buy Binusu Tokens?

You can acquire Binusu Tokens in three ways: through the Binusu Exchange (recommended), through the Changelly integration in your Binusu Wallet, or through a third-party exchange.

Buying Binusu Tokens via the Binusu Exchange:

This is the easiest and quickest way to buy Binusu Tokens without having to leave your Binusu Wallet especially if you already have crypto of stablecoins in your account.

Here are the steps:

  1. Log in to your Binusu account and add assets to your account.
  2. Go to the “Exchange” tab.
  3. Select the Binusu Token as the currency you want to purchase.
  4. Select the cryptocurrency, stablecoin or fiat currency you want to swap for Binusu Tokens.
  5. Tap “Exchange”.

Note: You can now also top up USDx, GBPx or EURx and swap them directly for Binusu with our Binusu/fiat pairs.

Buying Binusu Tokens via Changelly:

If you don’t already have assets in your Binusu account, you can still purchase tokens without leaving the Binusu Wallet through our Changelly integration. Simply follow the detailed steps below:

  1. Open the Binusu platform or the Binusu Wallet App and click on “Buy Binusu Tokens”
  2. Click the “Buy/Sell” button
  3. Provide the amount and the type of cryptocurrency you want to exchange for Binusu Tokens
  4. Click the “Swap Now” button
  5. Provide the email address you want to use and enter your Changelly password. (If you do not have an account at Changelly, then an account will be created on your behalf, using the email address you provided. You will receive a confirmation email.)
  6. You will be redirected to the “Payment” screen where you will see the Transaction ID, exchange amount, and your address.
  7. Click on the “Copy” button and then paste it in the “withdraw to” address field when creating the transaction using your private keys or Binusu Wallet.

You will receive an email confirming the transaction from Changelly to the email address you provided a couple of steps above. After the exchange process is completed, the Binusu Tokens will be automatically added to your Binusu Wallet.
