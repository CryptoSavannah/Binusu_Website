FAQs

How long will this promo last?

This limited-time promo will be available only from Thursday, May 5 at 00:00 UTC to Thursday, July 1 at 14:00 UTC.

How to top up assets to my Nexo account?

Adding assets to your Nexo account is quick and easy. Here are the different ways to top up:

  • Buy digital assets directly with your credit or debit card – our easiest and fastest option!
  • Transfer digital assets from an external wallet by using your private deposit address.
  • Top up funds via a wire transfer and swap them for crypto on the Nexo Exchange.

When will I get my $100 welcome bonus?

You will get your $100 reward within 30 days of passing Identity Verification and topping up the equivalent of $1,500 or more of any asset supported on the Nexo platform.

Example:

Let’s say you open your Nexo account on May 5, then you verify your identity and top up $1,500 on May 10. That means you’ll get rewarded with $100 worth of BTC by June 10.

How to get the additional bonus of $50 in BTC?

Want to earn even more? Simply swap at least $1,500 on the Nexo Exchange during the 30-day hold period and get an additional $50 in BTC.

Note: If you meet the criteria above, the additional $50 reward will land in your Nexo account between August 1 and August 10, 2022.

I can’t see my Bitcoin reward. Why?

You can always sign in to your account and check your reward status. Here are some reasons why you didn’t get your top-up bonus:

  • You didn’t hold the equivalent of $1,500 or more in supported assets for 30 days.
  • Your portfolio didn’t remain above $1,500 at the end of the 30-day holding period.
  • Also, keep in mind it could take up to 24 hours for the reward to show up inside your Nexo account after the 30-day period is over.

Can I get interest on or cash against my reward?

Once you receive your reward, you will automatically start receiving compound interest on your BTC – paid out daily.

You can also get funds against your top-up reward using our Instant Crypto Credit Lines or swap your BTC for other assets on the Nexo Exchange.
